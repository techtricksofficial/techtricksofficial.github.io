Make your windows command prompt terminal cool


Step 1 - Open the run window by pressing "windows + R" key on your keyboard


Step 2 - Type "cmd" on it and press enter to open command prompt.




Step 3 - Go to top of the window and right click on it and click on properties




Step 4 - Go to the font section. Then choose font size and your favourite font


Step 5 - Go to the color section and choose green as the color of screen text


Step 6 - Press OK and open the command prompt again, & enjoy the hacking interface!
